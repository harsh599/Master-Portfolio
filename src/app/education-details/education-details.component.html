My Academic Journey

Bachelor's Degree

My academic journey through Bharati Vidyapeeth University's Computer Engineering program was a dynamic odyssey of growth and achievement. Immersed in the realm of technology, I not only garnered a profound understanding of computer science's intricate nuances but also secured a coveted internship at a Fortune 500 company. My engagement extended to competitive coding and entrepreneurship contests, enriching my problem-solving skills. Crafting an Online Gym Booking platform showcased my entrepreneurial spirit, while my commitment to social responsibility found expression in waste reduction initiatives and community outreach. A 9.2 CGPA and diverse job offers solidified my dedication to excellence.


This chapter of my life illuminated the boundless potential of education, bridging theory and practice. As I step into the role of a Software Engineer, I carry forward this invaluable experience, a blend of technical proficiency and a passion for innovative solutions. Guided by the wisdom acquired, I am poised to create meaningful impact and embrace the ever-evolving world of technology.

Master's Degree

My pursuit of a Master's degree at Dalhousie University was ignited by a desire to break the monotony of remote work during the pandemic and embrace new challenges. Relocating to Canada in 2022, I embarked on a transformative journey that expanded my horizons. As a Masters of Applied Computer Science candidate, I was captivated by subjects like cloud computing, delving into intricate cloud architectures and large-scale serverless applications. This passion, coupled with sleepless nights and relentless dedication, translated into an impressive 3.7 GPA, showcasing my commitment to excellence.


Beyond academics, my time in Canada was marked by holistic growth. I thrived in a part-time role as a cashier, gaining firsthand insights into stress management and the value of work. Immersed in Canada's breathtaking landscapes and multicultural tapestry, I evolved into a more culturally aware and empathetic individual. With this enriched perspective, I'm ready to contribute as a Software Engineer, armed not only with technical proficiency but also with adaptability, resilience, and a global mindset cultivated during my Masters journey.
